Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 02, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Neduvaai (BoatNode) ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

Our App collects your device's precise location data to provide core functionality, including:

  • Navigation assistance at sea.
  • Safety monitoring and geofencing alerts.
  • Fleet management for boat owners and administrators.

Background Location: We may collect location data even when the App is closed or not in use if you have enabled safety monitoring features, to ensure your safety at sea and provide timely alerts to land administrators in case of emergencies.

1.2 Device Connection Data

The App connects to BoatNode hardware devices via Wi-Fi. We collect data related to this connection, including:

  • Wi-Fi SSID and signal strength.
  • Device status and telemetry received from the BoatNode hardware.

1.3 Camera Usage

We use your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes to facilitate easy joining of boats and pairing with devices. We do not record or store images or videos from your camera.

1.4 User Profile

When you create an account, we collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Phone number (for authentication)
  • Role (e.g., Boat Owner, Joiner)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality.
  • Ensure maritime safety through real-time monitoring and alerts.
  • Facilitate communication between boats and land stations.
  • Authenticate users and manage boat access.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • With Land Administrators: Location and status data is shared with authorized land administrators for safety monitoring and fleet management.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
